Malala Yousafzai

A Pakistani student and education activist.

"I truly believe the only way we can create global peace is through not only educating our minds, but our hearts and our souls."

-- Malala Yousafzai

This is a timeline of the life of Malala Yousafzai

  • July 12, 1997 - Born in Swat district, Pakistan.
  • Sep 1, 2008 - Malala has her first speech on " How dare the Taliban Take away my basic right of education".
  • Jan 2009 - Malala started blogging for the British Broadcasting Company Urdu using the pseudonym, Gul Makai.
  • Jan 1, 2011 - Malala wins the International Children's' Peace Prize
  • Jan 1, 2011 - Malala gets a death threat.
  • Jan 1, 2011 - Taliban had blown up two schools.
  • Oct 9, 2012 - Malala gets shot while travelling home from school by the Taliban gun man.
  • Jan 1, 2013 - Malala won the United Nations Human Rights Prize.
  • July 12, 2013 - Malala gave a speech at the United Nation. Her birthday becomes recognized as "Malala Day"
  • Oct 9, 2013 - Malala's memoir "I Am Malala" is released.
  • Oct 10, 2014 - Malala receives the Nobel Peace prize.
  • Jan 1, 2014 - Malala wins the Liberty Medal.
  • July 1, 2015 - Malala opened a girls school in Lebanon for refugees from the Syrian Civil War, with the support of The Malala Fund.
  • Oct 2, 2015 - Malala's documentary titled "He Named Me Malala" is released in theathers.
  • Feb 20, 2017 - Pakistani President launches a ten million education fund in Malala's honor.
  • Oct 2017 - Malala started studying at Oxford University.
  • May 2020 - Malala graduates from the University of Oxford.
